ABOUT ME


My name is Jonathan and I was born and raised in Sydney Australia. I studied media film and television back in University and previously worked at 21st Century Fox as well as Disney after their acquisition of the company. I mainly focused on the broadcast aspect where we'd recieve live feeds from around the world and redirect these feeds to the SEA region. This would include sports feeds, award shows and special events. Now here I am at Xccelerate underoing this course in hope of changing my career. I've always been interested in the world of I.T and the possibilities of coding seem to be endless, so it seemed like a no brainer to take this path.
